# otherElinmods

Or rather, "I cut my big ass modpack into pieces and added things to the pieces along the way."

Feat. 

Driders: PCC parts + race.

Drakons: PCC parts + race.

Fergmen: PCC parts + race.

Grotesqueries: PCC parts for more 'out there' pcs.

Clothing Variety Pack: PCC parts.

UTJunkbox: PCC parts and some of my attempts at furniture... (one lamp, one terrible snas bodypillow)

ManiJunk: more attempts at furniture. Some of them are fine. The statues and large plushie don't sit well unless altitude is adjusted and I can't figure it out. The cookies are able to be made but I can't figure out how to get their placement sprites right.

Jumi: Adds the Jumi race from Legend of Mana + modified vanilla bodies + two outfits (Elazul and Pearl's). Comes in Knight and Guardian flavor! Guardians cannot do damage. You will be a sitting duck if and when your allies die. Also adds LoM-style Sirens as a race (they have access to all songs and cries) and PCC parts. Includes an extra 'wings' for the normal back slot so they can be layered better with clothing but if your clothing is removed the wings on your body aren't, if that makes sense.
